# 2026 World Cup Fantasy — Group Stage Predictor

A browser-based fantasy game for the 2026 FIFA World Cup hosted by USA, Canada, and Mexico.

## How to Play

1. Open `world_cup_2026_fantasy.html` in any web browser
2. Pick **2 teams** from each of the 12 groups (A–L) to advance
3. Once all 12 groups are complete, hit **Submit Predictions**
4. Review your 24 advancing teams
